class RestaurantEmployee

Attributes :

  hoursWorked (float)
  serverCard (boolean)
  payRate (float)
  position (string)

Methods :

  takeSickDay (This would indicate if the employee took a sick day and affect the hoursWorked)
  payEmployee (This would calculate the employee's pay for the pay period. The attributes payRate and hoursWorked would be multiplied.)
  getServerCard (This is a boolean to indicate if the employee has their food server's card. It would determine the value of serverCard as true or false.)
  hireEmployee (This would give the employee a position and a payRate.)
